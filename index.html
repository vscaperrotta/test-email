AI VS PERSONAL DATA: IS BALANCE POSSIBLE IN PAYMENTS?

In the world of payments, artificial intelligence is a powerful tool for preventing fraud, enhancing user experiences, and automating complex processes. However, its use often involves accessing personal and sensitive data, raising concerns about privacy and security. Traditional AI models, typically cloud-based, process vast amounts of data externally, often beyond the company’s direct control. Today, new AI architectures based on on-premise and edge computing allow companies to process data locally, without transferring it to third parties. This enables businesses to harness the power of AI while safeguarding personal information, ensuring compliance with regulations like GDPR, and strengthening customer trust. This is the new frontier: AI with privacy at its core.
